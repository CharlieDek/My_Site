About
Charlie is from Durham, North Carolina, America's most tolerant city. He is currently a Stanford senior majoring in Symbolic Systems. Charlie performs stand-up comedy and improvisational comedy, and is the best improvisor in the world. He is a head writer for The Stanford Flipside and also writes for West and the Stanford Sitcom Project. Charlie has a great big imagination and great big dreams and he is very fond of his mother, Nancy.
